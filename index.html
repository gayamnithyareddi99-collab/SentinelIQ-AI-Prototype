Step 1

Shift Rota Upload

Before the shift begins, the operator or manager uploads their upcoming shift schedule. In Year 1, this is a simple CSV file or manual entry — no IT approval, no API integration, deployed within 48 hours.

CSV or Manual Entry
Upload a standard spreadsheet with: worker name, site ID, shift start/end time, lone-worker flag, and emergency contact. No special format — the platform accepts most common CSV layouts and auto-detects column headers.
Instant Validation
On upload, SentinelIQ AI instantly validates every row: checks for missing fields, duplicate entries, and lone-worker flags. Any issues are flagged with plain-English error messages before the shift is accepted into the system.
96 Check-In Intervals Programmed
For every shift uploaded, SentinelIQ AI automatically calculates and programs the check-in interval schedule (e.g. every 30 minutes for standard, every 20 minutes for HIGH RISK). All workers are notified in advance of their first check-in time.
Year 2 upgrade: Native API connectors for Planday, Deputy, and Rotacloud will auto-sync rotas in real time — eliminating the manual CSV step entirely. Zero IT approval required in Year 1.

Step 3

Manager Review & Decision

After risk screening, the platform notifies the relevant manager for every HIGH or MEDIUM risk shift. The manager sees the Risk Score, Confidence Level, plain-language explanation, and has three clear decision paths — all logged in the immutable compliance record.

Three Decision Paths Available
Add Staff
Manager adds a second worker to the shift. Risk Score is automatically re-calculated. If it drops below the HIGH threshold, standard monitoring applies.
Recalculated
Apply Override with Reason
If the shift must proceed with existing staffing, manager enters a mandatory reason. This is permanently logged in the HSE audit trail — legally defensible for inspectors and insurers. Enhanced check-in intervals activate automatically.
Reason logged
Cancel or Reschedule Shift
If neither option is viable, the shift is cancelled or rescheduled. The platform logs the cancellation reason and notifies all relevant parties. Insurance documentation is updated automatically.
Cancelled
Human sovereignty preserved. Managers retain full operational control at every step. The platform never locks out a decision — it enforces transparent accountability by requiring documented reasoning.

Step 4

Active Worker Check-In

During the shift, workers confirm their safety status via a 1–2 tap check-in on their mobile phone. The interface is deliberately simple — no logins, no gamification, no complexity. Workers receive immediate visible confirmation that their status is logged and supervisors are monitoring.

1-2 Tap Anti-Gamification Interface
A single large button on the worker's phone. Tap once to confirm safe status. Haptic + on-screen confirmation immediate. Designed to take under 3 seconds — not distract workers from their primary responsibilities during a high-pressure overnight shift.
Automated Reminder Before Due
SentinelIQ AI sends a gentle push notification 2 minutes before each check-in is due. This dramatically reduces missed check-ins caused by workers simply forgetting. In the pilot, this alone accounted for 80% of the improvement in compliance rate.
Offline Fallback — Works Without Signal
If the worker's phone loses signal, the check-in is queued locally and transmitted when connectivity is restored. The platform distinguishes between "no signal" (acceptable grace period) and "genuine miss" (escalate). Rural forecourts are fully supported.

Step 5

Intelligent Miss Detection

If a check-in is not received within the programmed interval, SentinelIQ AI does not immediately alarm — it applies an intelligent grace period calibrated to the site's connectivity profile, then triggers the escalation engine with full context.

T+0:00
Check-in due — platform waits for confirmation SMS / app response within programmed interval
T+0:30
Grace period — 30-second connectivity grace period applied for low-signal sites. Worker re-notified with escalating push alert tone.
T+1:00
Signal check — platform distinguishes "no signal" (logs with timestamp) from genuine non-response. Rural sites get extended grace period.
T+2:00
Confirmed Miss — check-in classified as genuine missed. Escalation engine activated. Worker status logged as UNCONFIRMED. Shift risk score elevated.
Average detection time: under 2 minutes. The previous manual process averaged 45 minutes from missed check-in to first supervisor notification. SentinelIQ AI reduces this by 96% — the most critical window for worker safety.

Active Safety Escalation & Forensics Engine

The ASEFE (Active Safety Escalation & Forensics Engine) deconstructs every missed check-in into a structured 5-tier escalation cascade. Each tier fires automatically if the previous tier receives no resolution — all within a pre-programmed time window.

T1
Automated Worker SMS Alert
"Your check-in is overdue. Please confirm you are safe by replying YES or tapping your app." Sent within 2 minutes of confirmed miss. No manual action required.
T+0:00 → Auto-fires on miss confirmation
T2
Supervisor / Area Manager Push Alert
Area Manager receives SMS + app push with: worker name, site location, current risk score, last confirmed status, and shift history. Full context in one notification.
T+2:12 → Auto-fires if T1 unresolved in 2 min
T3
Regional Backup Site Check
Regional duty manager notified. Backup wellness check protocol activated. CCTV access requested from site system. Compliance dossier preparation begins automatically in background.
T+10:00 → Auto-fires if T2 unresolved in 8 min
T4
Emergency Services Contact
999 call placed with pre-prepared incident brief: site address, worker details, risk profile, incident history, and all previous escalation actions taken. Response time minimised by full context upfront.
T+20:00 → Auto-fires if T3 unresolved in 10 min
T5

Step 7

Immutable HSE Audit Trail

Every action, decision, override, check-in, and escalation event is permanently recorded in a tamper-proof, timestamped audit trail. This is what makes SentinelIQ AI legally defensible — operators can demonstrate proactive lone-worker governance to HSE inspectors and commercial insurers on demand.

Tamper-Proof & Immutable
Once an entry is written to the audit trail, it cannot be edited, deleted, or backdated. Each record includes a cryptographic timestamp, the user ID who created it, and the full data payload. Any attempt to modify the record is itself logged.
HSE INDG73 & INDG424 Aligned
Every record maps directly to a specific requirement in the UK HSE lone-working guidelines. Manager override reason capture satisfies the "documented risk assessment" requirement. Escalation logs satisfy the "active monitoring" requirement.
Insurance Premium Negotiation
The complete audit trail — covering check-in compliance rates, escalation response times, override rationale, and incident outcomes — can be exported as a formatted PDF for commercial insurance underwriters. Pilot operators reported an estimated £3,200 annual premium saving.

Risk Mitigation Matrix

All Risk Vectors Addressed

Technical & Product Risks

False Sense of Safety Under Incomplete Data
Mitigation: Risk Score always paired with Confidence Level. If data is sparse or offline, confidence drops and managers are explicitly alerted. Platform never displays a risk score without a data quality indicator. Offline fallback templates and manual inputs ensure operational continuity under any infrastructure limitation.
Manager Distrust of Algorithm Decisions
Mitigation: Every risk score paired with a plain-language explanation (e.g. "HIGH RISK: Solo evening shift during peak historical fuel-theft window with limited local visibility"). Manager override feature preserved at all times — no lockouts. Managers retain full operational sovereignty.

Operational & Frontline Risks

Frontline Employee Engagement Friction
Mitigation: Anti-gamification UX philosophy. 1–2 tap check-in process. Real-time haptic and on-screen confirmation feedback ("Help has been requested — your supervisor is responding"). Worker interface focuses entirely on immediate, visible value — no badges, no leaderboards, no complexity.
POS & Rota Integration Barriers
Mitigation: Year 1 pilots bypass all API integrations via CSV and manual rota inputs. No corporate IT approval required. Deployment in 48 hours. Year 2 transitions to native integrations only after pilot validation and commercial conversion is secured.

Commercial & Market Risks

Long Enterprise Sales Cycles & Multi-Stakeholder Inertia
Mitigation: Direct outreach to secure 3–5 initial pilot customers. Strict 8–12 week ROI gating window. Multi-buyer value mapping tailored simultaneously to Operations, HR, Risk, and Finance decision-makers to build cross-functional commercial buy-in from day one. Pilot agreement requires upfront written commitment to paid conversion on verified metrics.
Pilot-to-Paid Conversion Failure
Mitigation: Strict commercial pilot gating — operators must sign conditional LOIs committing to paid B2B SaaS conversion upon 4 verified success metrics. No open-ended free trials permitted. Pre-defined success thresholds: under 3-minute escalation, 98%+ check-in compliance, 100% override logging.

Regulatory & Compliance Risks

UK HSE Regulatory Non-Compliance
Mitigation: Escalation pathways mapped directly to INDG73 and INDG424. Manager override reason capture creates legally defensible audit trail. Platform designed compliance-first — every feature traceable to a regulatory requirement. 100% override logs provide HSE inspectors with verifiable evidence of proactive safety governance.
UK GDPR & ICO Data Privacy Exposure
Mitigation: Privacy-by-design architecture. No continuous location tracking, audio surveillance, or biometric monitoring. All employee data processed using encrypted protocols in full ICO alignment. Strict data minimisation — scheduling metadata only. Employee privacy protected while maintaining robust operational safety compliance.
