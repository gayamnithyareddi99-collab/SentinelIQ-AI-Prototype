Root Causes of the Safety Gap
No Proactive Safety Monitoring
Current systems rely on post-incident reporting. Workers in distress must self-initiate contact impossible in confrontational situations.
Manual Check-In Failures

Frequently Asked Questions
Is there a minimum contract length?
We offer rolling monthly contracts for Logic Core and Logic Sovereign. DNA Architect plans include a 12-month minimum to ensure full AI model training and ROI realisation. All plans include a 30-day free pilot with no obligation.
Do I need to replace my existing CCTV or alarm system?
No. SentinelIQ AI works alongside your existing CCTV, alarm, and POS systems. The DNA Architect plan includes API integration. Logic Core and Sovereign operate independently via app and SMS with no hardware changes needed.
How does the 30-day pilot work?
You get full access to the Logic Sovereign platform for 30 days at no cost. We configure the system for your site, train your team, and provide a full performance report at the end of the pilot. No credit card required.
Is SentinelIQ AI HSE compliant?
Yes. All SentinelIQ AI plans automatically generate RIDDOR-aligned incident logs and duty-of-care records. Our platform has been reviewed against HSE lone-worker guidelines and is designed to meet or exceed statutory requirements for UK forecourt operators.
What happens if a worker doesn't check in?
SentinelIQ AI initiates a 4-tier escalation protocol: (1) automated SMS re-prompt, (2) app push alert with 2-min window, (3) site manager phone call, (4) emergency services auto-brief and contact if no response. Average resolution time in pilot: 2.2 minutes.

Regulatory Compliance & Risk Management

SentinelIQ AI is built around UK regulatory requirements for lone-worker safety. We help operators meet their legal obligations under the Health and Safety at Work Act 1974, Management of Health and Safety at Work Regulations 1999, and RIDDOR 2013.

HSE Lone Worker Guidelines

Full compliance with Health and Safety Executive lone-worker guidance. SentinelIQ AI satisfies all welfare monitoring, communication, and emergency protocol requirements.

RIDDOR 2013 Auto-Logging

Automatic generation of RIDDOR-compliant incident records. Every check-in, escalation, and override is timestamped and logged to meet statutory reporting requirements.

GDPR Data Protection

Worker data encrypted at rest and in transit. GDPR-compliant data retention policies. No biometric data collected. Privacy-by-design architecture aligned with ICO guidance.

Business Risk Register
Regulatory Change Risk
Future changes to HSE lone-worker regulations could require platform updates. Mitigation: active monitoring of legislative pipeline; modular compliance architecture allows rapid updates.
Data Security & Breach Risk
Loss of worker personal data could cause reputational damage and regulatory fines. Mitigation: AES-256 encryption, penetration testing, Cyber Essentials certification, ISO 27001 roadmap.
Adoption Resistance
Independent operators may resist new technology or perceive cost barrier. Mitigation: 30-day free pilot, onboarding support, and ROI calculator demonstrating insurance premium savings.
Escalation False Positives
